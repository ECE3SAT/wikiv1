PAYLOAD



The payload was developed in parallel of the 4 modules:

  • On Board Computer (OBC)

  • Energy Power Supply (EPS)

  • Attitude Determination Control System (ADCS)

  • Telecommunication System (TCS)

The goal of the payload is to test in practice a physical theory who was validated as a new deorbiting system.The launch is scheduled for the end of 2021. If the mission is a success this can lead to great progress on larger satellites.
